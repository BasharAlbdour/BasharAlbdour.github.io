Who I Am

I am an Energy Engineer with a Bachelor's degree from the German Jordanian University, currently pursuing a Master's in Environmental and Renewable Energy Engineering at GJU. My background spans solar PV system design, AV pre-sales, building automation, and electrical engineering across Jordan, Saudi Arabia, and Germany .

I am now channelling that same engineering rigour and commitment to continuous learning into applied AI and machine learning through the AI.SPIRE program — progressing systematically from data pipelines and SQL through machine learning, NLP, retrieval-augmented generation, and full production deployment.
